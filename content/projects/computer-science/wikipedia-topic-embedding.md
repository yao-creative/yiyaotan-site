[Computer Science Projects](/projects/computer-science/) 

Web-scraped Wikipedia article summary paragraphs, tokenized them, and vectorized them into embeddings. Performed various clustering techniques like Ward’s algorithm, PCA, and t-SNE to visualize the embedding space of topics.
